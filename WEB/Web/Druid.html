Classes
Druid
Gameplay
The Druid is a shapeshifter class, and can change forms at will. They also have access to earth and storm magic. Different skills utilize different forms.
Storm magic persists on the battlefield, so players able to unleash torrents of lightning, wind, and rain before shifting forms to ravage foes.
